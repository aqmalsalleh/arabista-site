Chapter III · Heritage

We spin our own thread.

Founded in 2019 in Kuala Lumpur, Arabista is a vertically integrated maison. We do not outsource craft. From the sourcing of authentic Nidha Dubai cloth to the final stitch on a hemline, every gesture remains within our house.

Our retail collection and our alteration ateliers are not separate ventures — they are one continuous discipline. The hands that finish the collection are the hands that keep it in service.
