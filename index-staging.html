About Arabista

Made in-house. End to end.

Founded in 2020 in Kuala Lumpur, Arabista is a fully in-house brand. We source authentic Nidha Dubai fabric, stitch every abaya ourselves, and run our own nationwide alteration hubs — no outsourcing at any step.

That means the same tailors who craft our collection are the ones who service your wardrobe — so every alteration meets the same premium standard as a new piece.
