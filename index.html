Our Craftsmanship, Our Story

Founded in 2019, Arabista Enterprise is a proud Bumiputera brand specializing in black abayas. Inspired by Middle Eastern fashion but rooted in Malaysian values, we design abayas that empower women to express faith and elegance without compromise.

Each piece is meticulously crafted using premium Nidha Dubai fabric, cut by skilled seamstresses, and finished with practical features that fit modern lifestyles. From Malaysia to the world, we are committed to making Arabista synonymous with the word abaya.
