Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Arabista Enterprise ("Arabista", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use our website, alteration tracking services, and automated WhatsApp communication channels. We are committed to ensuring your privacy is protected and compliant with applicable data protection laws, including the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

1. Information We Collect

To provide our alteration and logistics services, we collect the following necessary information:

  • Contact Information: Phone numbers and names used for WhatsApp communication and order identification.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates, street addresses, and postal codes required for Lalamove pickup and delivery routing.
  • Service Data: Garment types, measurement specifications, and alteration preferences.
  • Financial Data: Transaction statuses and IDs. (Note: We do not store credit card or banking details; these are processed securely by SenangPay).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly to facilitate your requested services:

  • To assign your alteration order to the nearest available Arabista Tailor Hub.
  • To provide real-time status updates and live tracking via our web portal and WhatsApp.
  • To generate accurate logistics quotes and route Lalamove riders to your location.
  • To process payments securely.

3. Third-Party Integrations

We operate a decentralized logistics ecosystem. To fulfill your order, we securely share specific, limited data with verified third-party processors:

Meta (WhatsApp) Used strictly to send order confirmations and live updates. Meta acts as a data processor.
Lalamove API Your address and phone number are shared exclusively for pickup and delivery routing.
SenangPay Securely processes your transactions. We never touch your raw banking data.
Tailor Hubs Your specific alteration requirements are shared with your assigned tailor.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to marketing agencies.

4. WhatsApp Consent

By initiating a tracking request or messaging the Arabista WhatsApp bot, you explicitly consent to receive transactional updates and customer service replies via WhatsApp. You may opt-out of notifications at any time by requesting an order cancellation or notifying our support team.

5. Security & Retention

We implement strict security measures, including HMAC-SHA256 cryptographic signatures for financial API calls and secure token middleware, to protect against unauthorized access. We retain your alteration measurements and contact data only as long as necessary to provide future alteration services or to comply with legal and accounting obligations.
